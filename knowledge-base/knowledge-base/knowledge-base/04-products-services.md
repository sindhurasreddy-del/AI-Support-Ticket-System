NOAVIA — Products & Services Guide
Service Overview
NOAVIA delivers three core service lines to German B2B companies:
1. NOAVIA AI Agents
Custom-built AI agents deployed for specific business functions:
Support Ticket Agents: Automatically classify, prioritize, and draft responses to incoming customer tickets. Ideal for companies like Müller Maschinenbau or Weber Elektrotechnik handling high volumes of technical inquiries.
Document Processing Agents: Extract, classify, and route information from PDFs, invoices, contracts, and compliance documents. Popular with Feinchemie and Steuerberatung clients.
Knowledge Assistants: Internal Q&A agents that answer employee questions using company documentation. Deployed on-premise for clients with strict data requirements.
2. NOAVIA Workflow Automation
End-to-end automation of business processes using n8n and custom integrations:
Order processing and ERP integration
Automated invoice handling and payment reconciliation
Shipment tracking and logistics notifications (e.g., Fischer Logistik use case)
Quality control reporting for manufacturing clients
Employee onboarding workflow automation
3. NOAVIA Consulting & Strategy
Strategic consulting for companies planning AI adoption:
AI readiness assessment (2-day workshop)
Technology stack evaluation and recommendation
RAG (Retrieval-Augmented Generation) architecture design
Multi-agent system design for complex organizations
GDPR compliance review for AI deployments
Vendor selection support (LLM providers, vector databases, hosting)
Deployment Options
Cloud-hosted: NOAVIA manages infrastructure on German/EU data centers (Hetzner, Frankfurt)
On-premise: Full deployment on client infrastructure. Required for clients in regulated industries (Chemie, Steuerberatung)
Hybrid: Core infrastructure on-premise, AI model inference via EU-hosted API endpoints
Technology Stack
NOAVIA solutions are built with:
n8n for workflow orchestration
Qdrant or Weaviate for vector search
OpenAI GPT-4 or local LLMs (Mistral, Llama) for AI inference
Docker for containerized deployment
PostgreSQL for structured data storage
SLA (Service Level Agreement)
Standard SLA: 99.5% uptime, 4-hour response time for critical issues
Enterprise SLA: 99.9% uptime, 1-hour response time, 24/7 dedicated support
