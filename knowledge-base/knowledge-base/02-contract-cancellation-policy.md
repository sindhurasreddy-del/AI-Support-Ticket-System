NOAVIA — Contract & Cancellation Policy
Contract Terms
All NOAVIA client engagements are governed by individual service agreements. Standard contract durations are:
Pilot projects: 4–8 weeks, fixed scope and pricing
Standard contracts: 12 months, renewable annually
Enterprise agreements: Custom duration (typically 24–36 months)
Cancellation Policy
Pilot projects: May be terminated at any time with 7 days written notice. Fees for work already completed are non-refundable.
Standard contracts: May be cancelled with 90 days written notice before the end of the contract period. Early termination incurs a fee of 25% of the remaining contract value.
Enterprise agreements: Cancellation terms are specified in the individual service agreement.
To cancel a contract, send written notice to vertraege@noavia.de with your Kundennummer (customer number) and company name.
Refund Policy
Within 14 days of contract signing: Full refund if no development work has commenced.
After project delivery: Refunds are evaluated case-by-case. If delivered work does not meet agreed specifications, NOAVIA will remediate at no additional cost.
Monthly retainer services: No refunds for partial months. Cancellation takes effect at the end of the current billing cycle.
Data After Contract End
After contract termination, your data (AI models, workflow configurations, processed documents) is retained for 90 days. During this period, you can request a full data export in standard formats (JSON, CSV). After 90 days, all client data is permanently deleted in accordance with GDPR. You may request earlier deletion by contacting datenschutz@noavia.de.
Transition Support
When a contract ends, NOAVIA provides up to 10 hours of free transition support to help migrate your workflows to an alternative provider or bring them in-house. This includes documentation handover and a final knowledge transfer session.
