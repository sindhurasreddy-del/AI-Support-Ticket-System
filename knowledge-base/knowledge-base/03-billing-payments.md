NOAVIA — Billing & Payments
Payment Methods
We accept the following payment methods:
SEPA-Überweisung / Bank transfer (preferred for all German clients)
SEPA-Lastschrift (direct debit, available for retainer agreements)
Credit card (Visa, MasterCard) for pilot projects under €10,000
Billing Structure
Pilot projects: 50% upfront upon contract signing, 50% upon project delivery
Standard contracts: Monthly invoicing, payable within the agreed Zahlungsziel (payment term)
Enterprise agreements: Quarterly invoicing or as specified in the service agreement
Payment Terms (Zahlungsziel)
Standard payment terms vary by client agreement:
14 Tage (14 days) — standard for smaller engagements and new clients
30 Tage (30 days) — standard for established clients (Stammkunden)
45 Tage (45 days) — available for Großkunden (large accounts) by arrangement
60 Tage (60 days) — reserved for Enterprise clients with signed framework agreements
Invoices
All invoices are issued in EUR and comply with German invoicing requirements (§14 UStG). Invoices are sent electronically to the billing email on file and are also available in the client portal at https://portal.noavia.de under Rechnungen (Invoices).
Late Payments
If a payment is overdue:
A friendly reminder is sent after 7 days past the due date
A formal Mahnung (payment reminder) is sent after 14 days
After 30 days overdue, support services may be temporarily suspended
After 60 days, the matter is escalated to our accounts department
Tax Information
All prices quoted by NOAVIA are net prices (excluding VAT). German Umsatzsteuer of 19% is applied for clients within Germany. EU business clients with a valid USt-IdNr. (VAT ID) are exempt under the reverse charge mechanism. Please ensure your VAT ID is correctly registered in our system.
