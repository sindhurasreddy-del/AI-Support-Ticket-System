NOAVIA — Frequently Asked Questions
What does NOAVIA do?
NOAVIA is a German AI solutions company that builds AI-powered automation agents and intelligent workflow systems for mid-sized B2B companies across the DACH region. We specialize in deploying custom AI agents for industries including Maschinenbau, Logistik, Chemie, Elektrotechnik, Metallverarbeitung, and more.
What industries does NOAVIA serve?
We serve a wide range of German B2B industries including:
Maschinenbau (e.g., production workflow automation)
Transport & Logistik (e.g., shipment tracking agents)
Chemie & Feinchemie (e.g., compliance document processing)
Elektrotechnik & Gebäudetechnik (e.g., maintenance ticket routing)
Metallverarbeitung & Feinmechanik (e.g., quality control automation)
Lebensmittel & Verpackung (e.g., order processing agents)
IT-Dienstleistung & Steuerberatung (e.g., document classification)
Immobilien & Baustoffhandel (e.g., tenant inquiry automation)
